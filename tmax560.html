tmax 560

Quoting Greatbikers page, Wednesday, March 13, the 2024 TMAX is equipped with a variety of new features that can support and provide comfort to the users. The features included include a motion control system, a road traffic control system, a color TFT display, a Garmin navigation system, a cruise control system, and a smart key system with remote control. Not only that, the 2024 TMAX is also equipped with heated seats, which provides extraordinary comfort. Yamaha TMAX has always been known for its practices, such as the already installed front protection, and improving the vision of the drivers of the car. In addition, the large storage compartments under the seats allow for storing a variety of essential items. The presence of the LED headlight system adds a modern and proportional impression, in addition to improving the vision of the driver of the car. As for the engine, the 2024 Yamaha TMAX 560 is equipped with a 562cc engine with liquid cooling and fuel injection. This engine is capable of producing 47 horsepower at 7500 rpm and a maximum rotation speed of 55.5 Nm.
